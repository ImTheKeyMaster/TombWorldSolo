    AI REFERENCE

    Decision Logic

    01

    Select the threat

    Prioritize an NPO that can attack, interfere with the mission, exploit an exposed operative, or reach the closest player.

    02

    Follow behavior

    Brawlers close and fight. Marksmen seek clear shots. Sentinels fight nearby threats, then bring ranged weapons to bear.

    03

    Break ties against the player

    Choose the result most likely to incapacitate a player operative or prevent mission success. Randomize only true ties.

    04

    Resolve on the tabletop

    The app provides order, movement, target, action sequence, and a visual attack roll. Apply defence and special rules from the datacards you are using.

    ROSTER EDITOR

    Add NPO

    Attack values power the visual dice roller. Adjust them to match the datacard or profile you are using.

    PLAYER ATTACK RESOLVER

    Attack NPO

    Enter the player attack result, then roll the NPO's saves.
